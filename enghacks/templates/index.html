Our Inspiration

The previous problem with financial decision making was lack of information; with the advent of the internet, we faced with other problems now whereby there is so much information that we can't factor it all in our decision making, i.e. we suffer from bounded rationality. Additionally, having to rely on other consulting companies limits our agency to make these decisions ourselves. Lastly, going the long-short selling way often renders our investments a zero sum game and the need to have a long term money-making strategy was never so pertinent.

That is why we have created this tool. Having trained our machine model on data from SNP500 companies to find out what factors contribute to long term dividend maximization, we make it easier for you to assess how you can make the best investments to maximize your gain.
